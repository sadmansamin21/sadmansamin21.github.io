Peer-Indexed Research

  • Effects of Rice Husk Ash on Concrete Compressive Strength: A Systematic Review

    Civil Engineering Sustainability SCM Published 2026 · Zenodo · DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20241511

    Systematic review and quantitative synthesis evaluating partial cement replacement with rice husk ash (RHA). Uses a random-effects framework. Evaluates curing duration, particle fineness, and replacement percentages (5–10% vs. >15%) and their influence on concrete performance.

  • Systematic Survey of Machine Learning and Deep Learning for Natural Disaster Prediction and Early Warning Systems in South Asia (2020–2025)

    Machine Learning Deep Learning Disaster Management Published 2026 · SSRN · DOI: 10.2139/ssrn.6786939

    Multi-hazard systematic review of 42 peer-reviewed studies across flood, cyclone, earthquake, and landslide domains. Features a validation integrity audit: 37.5% of evaluated seismic papers suffer from temporal data leakage due to flawed random splitting protocols.

  • The Economics of Artificial Intelligence and Labor

    AI Economics Labor Markets Macro ~10,000 words · Reference handbook · 2026

    Comprehensive multi-disciplinary synthesis of labor economics, macroeconomics, organizational science, and international development around the question of what generative AI does to work. Covers the task-based framework (Acemoglu-Restrepo), GPT economics, the "Four Cs" constraint model for LMICs (Connectivity, Compute, Context, Competency), premature de-professionalization in developing nations, and Bangladesh-specific RMG sector vulnerability.
