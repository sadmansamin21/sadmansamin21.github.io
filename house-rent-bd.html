🏠 About This Tool

House Rent Estimator Bangladesh provides estimated monthly rent ranges for 200+ locations across all 8 divisions of Bangladesh — including neighborhoods inside Dhaka like Dhanmondi, Banasree, Gulshan, Mirpur, and many more.

Rent estimates are based on a 2-bedroom standard apartment. Actual prices may vary based on apartment size, floor, building age, furnishing, and landlord preferences.
