🗺️ About This Tool

Land Price Estimator provides estimated land prices per katha (1.65 decimal / ~67 sq m) for 200+ locations across Dhaka city, Dhaka Division, and major districts of Bangladesh.

Prices reflect median residential/commercial land market rates. Actual prices vary based on road width, plot size, land use type, legal clearance, and negotiation.

Data reflects 2024–2025 market estimates. 1 Katha = 1.65 Decimal = ~720 sq ft.
