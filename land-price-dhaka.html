🗺️ About This Tool

Land Price Estimator provides estimated land prices per katha (1.65 decimal / ~67 sq m) specifically for Dhaka City and surrounding districts within the Dhaka Division.

Prices reflect median residential/commercial land market rates. Actual prices vary heavily based on road width, corner plots, legal clearance, and direct negotiation.

Data reflects 2024–2025 market estimates. 1 Katha = 1.65 Decimal = ~720 sq ft.
